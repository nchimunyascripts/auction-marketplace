About

This project, borne out of my personal experiences and fueled by my fervor for platforms like eBay and Auction.com, has been a remarkable journey from inception to reality. What began as a modest venture, characterized by a barebones console application, has evolved into a substantial endeavor, now boasting a full-fledged GUI.

As part of my commitment to excellence at Holberton School, this endeavor has not only honed my technical prowess but also allowed me to channel my passion and creativity into a tangible manifestation of my abilities.

From the outset, this project was a means to encapsulate the essence of online auctions and commerce, inspired by the transformative impact of platforms that revolutionized the way we buy and sell. The concept initially took shape in the form of a rudimentary console application, serving as a foundation for the bigger vision that lay ahead.

As the project transitioned into a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, the scope expanded exponentially. The metamorphosis from a simple console application to a feature-rich, user-friendly GUI was driven by an unyielding determination to showcase not only technical aptitude but also the capacity to blend functionality with aesthetics.

Through meticulous development, intricate design considerations, and an unwavering commitment to detail, this project is a testament to my journey as a developer. It underscores my evolution from a novice programmer to an aspiring professional, ready to navigate the complex terrain of software development.

I am immensely proud to present this project as the culmination of my efforts. Its transformation from a concept inspired by personal encounters with online auctions into a tangible creation reflects my dedication to mastering the craft of programming. The project, conceived and brought to life by me, Joseph Nchimunya, stands as an embodiment of my journey, my aspirations, and my commitment to the world of software engineering.

Key Features

Auction Management

Allow users to manage their own auctions, including editing the auction details, extending the end date/time if desired, and closing the auction once it has ended.

Auction Listing

Provide a platform for users to create and list their items for auction. Users should be able to specify details such as the title, description, starting bid, and end date/time for each auction.

Watchlist

Provide a platform for users to create and list their items for auction. Users should be able to specify details such as the title, description, starting bid, and end date/time for each auction.
